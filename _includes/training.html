Training that really moves you ahead

Good training can be infuiatingly hard to find, good training on functional programming topics even more so. When you don't even know the right questions to ask it feels like wandering around an unfamiliar landscape with no map. That's why our training starts where you're at, in a language you're familiar with, and introduces new concepts one at a time so you're never lost in the woods.

Our workshops are hands-on affairs. The only way to really learn is by doing, so you'll spend lots of your time working with new paradigms and techniques. By the time you're done, you'll have a strong foundation to pursue your own independent learning. All of our course materials are provided to you to take home, and you'll be invited to join our Slack community with all the others who have embarked upon this journey of exploration.
